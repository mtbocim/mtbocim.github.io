About Me

A little about myself:

I've been repairing musical instrument for nearly a decade, and involved in music in someway going even further back to when I was young. Now I'm looking to move into the world of the web.

Puzzles and logic have always fascinated me. I grew up with legos and Myst. Instead of doing my math homework, I tried to write programs in my ti-83 to do it for me. Since then coding has manifested in various places throughout my adulthood. My favorite bit of code I wrote to date was a music generating program for a music theory class. I'm proud to say I recieved an A on that project.

I enjoy patterns and structure, wherever they may surface, and strive to incorporate them into my life.

In my downtime, I'm 100% a hobbyist, occasional jazz performer, cad drawing to 3d printing creator, pc and board gamer, origami folder, nature photographer.
