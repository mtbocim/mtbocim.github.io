A little about myself:

I've been repairing musical instrument for nearly a decade, and looking to move into the programming world (see resume below). I've been dabbling in logic and coding since high school on my ti-83.

My favorite bit of code I ever wrote was a program I wrote in college. It used music rules to generate a text file which was then read by a music notation program and transcribed (I'm proud to say I recieved an A on that project).

In my downtime, I'm 100% a hobbyist. 3d printing and cad drawing, pc and board gaming, origami, nature photography. Really, I think I just like patterns and structure, wherever that may surface.
